About

What we offer.

  • Accurate Information

    First, an accurate inventory of properties is created with their attributes and assign them to contractors.

  • Communication

    Contractors are notified as properties are assigned, and removed from their schedule. When trash and other pickup items are left on the curbside it is communicated with a single click from the Mark Mowed screen.

  • Efficient Billing

    What has been mowed is easily entered since the mowing schedule and billing screen are in the same order. Invoicing is just a few clicks and it is done.

  • Quick Invoice Processing and Payment

    Since only the properties that are assigned show up on the invoice along with the correct amount for each property, verifying invoices is a breeze.

  • Compliance

    As properties are marked mowed, some are selected for compliance. Communication to the inspectors and if necessary to the contractor is effortless.

  • Reporting

    Monitoring and managing Contractor and Inspector productivity is a snap.
